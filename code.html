Revolve Studios Mimic

Revolve Studios is a creative agency in Portland, Oregon, who create design work that I greatly admire. In order to improve my front-end development skills, I decided to code my own version of one of their works using only the image that they provide on their dribbble account as a reference.
I don't have access to their images or typefaces of course, so I found my images: here, here, here, & here. The typefaces, I found on Google Fonts From which I used Hind, Montserrat, and Playfair Display.
